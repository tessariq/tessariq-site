## Angle

Contrast proprietary agent dashboards with repo-local, scriptable artifacts that survive product churn and can be automated against.

## Outline

### 1) What dashboards are good at

- short-term observation
- convenience

### 2) What they are bad at

- portability
- long-term inspectability
- integration into existing workflows

### 3) Why files win operationally

- readable without vendor tooling
- composable with scripts
- archive-friendly

### 4) The nuanced position

Dashboards can exist, but they should not become the only source of truth.

### 5) Closing

The durable substrate should be files first, UI second.
