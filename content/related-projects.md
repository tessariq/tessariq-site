## Shared direction

Together, these projects explore different parts of the same space:

- safe execution and promotion,
- workflow structure and task discipline,
- review quality and evaluator behavior,
- benchmark design across models and harnesses.

Each project has its own scope and maturity, but all of them aim at a more inspectable, evidence-based approach to agentic engineering.
