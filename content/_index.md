AI-assisted engineering is quietly becoming the memory, the planner, the operator, and the reviewer of your work. Every layer you hand over is a layer you can't easily take back.

Tessariq is built on a simple belief: your workflow, your artifacts, and your operational memory should not depend on the incentives of any single vendor. It keeps specs, evidence, and handoffs in open formats — Markdown, JSON, YAML — so workflows remain portable and agents remain replaceable.

It's also a lab. A place to experiment with how agents and developers can work together, safely and transparently, without surrendering the engineering process to a black box.
